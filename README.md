Banking Behavioural Score Card Model: Predicting the credit risk of the customers with savings account in the bank.

Getting Started: The objective is to build a banking behaviour scorecard model for bank's internal customers through a user's liability account and predict the credit risk.

Key: The data is of low transacting customers Customer risk profile means the probability of the customer defaulting on his EMI. Evaluation: F1-Score

Prerequisites(Things to install prior to using the notebook.): numpy,pandas,matplotlib,seaborn,pytorch,xgboost

Data description: Total variables: 2395 Col1: ID Col2: Target Variable

What you will learn: Handling huge number of columns Working with imbalanced data Feature selection Dimensionality reduction Algorithms - XGBoost, Catboost, and LightGBM Oversampling techniques such as SMOTE, SVM SMOTE, Borderline SMOTE, and ADASYN.
